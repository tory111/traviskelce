Travis Kelce is a prominent American professional football tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs. Drafted by the Chiefs in 2013, he has remained with the team throughout his career and is considered one of the greatest tight ends in NFL history. Kelce has contributed to the Chiefs winning three Super Bowls. He is recognized for his athletic abilities and understanding of zone coverages. He holds multiple NFL record.
